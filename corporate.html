What's in it for the Corporate Participants

Corporate registrants will be able to attend any/all speakers, tours and panel discussions as the student registrants. Corporate registrants will also have the option of purchasing the compiled resume book of student attendees. Corporations will have the option of purchasing a booth at the expo which will allow the corporation to showcase themselves to the conference attendees as well as all other students at the Unversity of Illinois, and any other students who wish to attend.
